# Giant Dragon Egg

A mod I made for a friend of mine who wanted a bigger dragon egg!

## Description

This mod replaces the standard Ender Dragon egg with a massive 2x2x2 giant dragon egg. When placed in the world, the egg automatically expands, filling out its massive shape with accurate hitboxes and collisions.

The egg functions exactly like the standard one:
- Hitting it in survival will make it randomly teleport, taking up a new 2x2x2 space!
- Breaking blocks underneath it will cause the massive egg to succumb to gravity and fall.
- When falling onto non-solid blocks (like torches or slabs), it will pop back into a standard dragon egg item.
- When placed in **The End** dimension on top of a Bedrock block (like the exit portal), it will retain its normal 1x1 size. Anywhere else, or in any other dimension, it's GIANT!

## Compatibility

This mod supports:
- **Forge 1.21**
- **NeoForge 1.21**
- **Fabric 1.21**

## Localization

The mod includes built-in translations for over 30 languages (including Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Russian, and more). The Giant Dragon Egg will display correctly in your native language across all supported mod loaders.
